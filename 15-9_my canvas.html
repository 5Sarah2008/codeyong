Small boats are typically found on inland waterways such as rivers and lakes, or in protected coastal areas.

girl

A girl is a young female human, usually a child or an adolescent. When a girl becomes an adult, she is accurately described as a woman. However, the term girl is also used for other meanings, including young woman, and is sometimes used as a synonym for daughter, or girlfriend.
